Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time with no questions asked. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
Is AUREX SEBI-registered investment advice?+
No. AUREX generates AI-powered research reports for educational and informational purposes only. Our reports are tools to help you make informed decisions — they are not registered investment advice. Always consult a SEBI-registered advisor before making investment decisions.
How is AUREX different from Moneycontrol or Screener.in?+
Moneycontrol and Screener.in give you raw data — P/E ratios, balance sheets, charts. You still have to interpret everything yourself. AUREX gives you decisions — it tells you whether that P/E is justified, what your stop-loss should be, how the stock correlates with your other holdings, and exactly what percentage of your portfolio to allocate. That's the fundamental difference.
How often does AUREX update its AI models?+
AUREX runs monthly AI model updates on the 1st of every month. This refreshes the market context, sector analysis frameworks and screening parameters automatically. Pro Annual subscribers get priority access to new framework updates before other tiers.
Can I use AUREX on my mobile phone?+
Yes! AUREX is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On Android, tap the browser menu and select "Add to Home Screen." On iPhone, tap Share then "Add to Home Screen." The app installs like a native app with the AUREX icon and works offline too.
What payment methods are accepted?+
We accept all UPI apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), Visa, Mastercard, and all Indian debit/credit cards. Simply scan the QR code or tap your preferred UPI app in the payment modal. No international transaction fees for Indian users.
