Frequently Asked Questions

Why is AUREX priced higher than other tools?+
AUREX is not a data tool — it's an intelligence platform. We merge Goldman Sachs, Bridgewater, JPMorgan and BlackRock frameworks into one AI system that gives you decisions, not just numbers. Bloomberg Terminal costs ₹1,75,000+ per year and gives you raw data. AUREX Pro at ₹2,999/month gives you complete institutional analysis with AI-generated answers directly in-app. The price reflects the value: institutional research that used to cost lakhs, now accessible for thousands.
Does the AI answer appear directly in the app?+
Yes — this is what makes AUREX fundamentally different from any other AI financial tool. When you click FIRE, the AI processes your complete profile and generates the full Goldman + Bridgewater + JPMorgan + BlackRock report directly inside the app. No copy-pasting prompts into ChatGPT. No external tools. No redirects. The answer appears right here — formatted, structured and ready to act on.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime with no questions asked. You retain access until the end of your billing period.
Is AUREX SEBI-registered investment advice?+
No. AUREX generates AI-powered research for educational and informational purposes only. Our reports are tools to help you make informed decisions — not registered investment advice. Always consult a SEBI-registered advisor before making investment decisions.
How is AUREX secured against hacking or data theft?+
AUREX uses multiple layers of security: Content Security Policy headers blocking unauthorized script injection, HSTS forcing HTTPS-only connections, X-Frame-Options preventing clickjacking, input sanitization on all fields preventing XSS and SQL injection, rate limiting on all AI API calls, no persistent storage of sensitive financial data, and session tokens that expire after 30 days. We take security as seriously as institutional finance firms do.
How often does AUREX update its AI models?+
AUREX runs monthly AI model updates on the 1st of every month — refreshing market context, sector frameworks and screening parameters automatically. Institutional subscribers get priority access to new framework updates before other tiers.
